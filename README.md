# DataPanino

<P> Purpose: Provide historical data models and graphs showing World Bank API data for France, Germany, Italy, Netherlands, Spain, Switzerland, UK as top 7 leading contributors of intellectual property (IP) sales in European Union (EU). Data available in most cases from 1975 to 2015. Show impact of IP sales in context of 3 other economic indicators: GDP, GNI, and FDI. Show impact of foreign direct investments (FDI) on one country's economy, and calculate percentage as it relates to intellectual property patents. Provide instrument for economic analysis to compare growth of 2 countires in the EU. Map the corridors of highest IP sales output in 7 countries to show which geographic centers show greatest impact on economic development for intellectual property in high technology sector.</p>
